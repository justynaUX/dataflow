Business Context & Brief

Understanding the problem space before touching any design tool

The Platform

DataFlow is a B2B SaaS platform for customer relationship management (CRM) and sales analytics. Clients are medium and large enterprises. Primary users are Sales Managers.

The Problem

Users report data overload. They have hundreds of tables and charts but struggle to draw concrete conclusions:

  • —Which leads should I focus on?
  • —Why did sales in region X drop?
  • —What actions should I take tomorrow morning?
The Task

Design a new feature: AI Smart Assistant & Insight Panel — the application's central point that not only displays data but interprets it and suggests actions using AI.

Evaluation Criteria
  • —Product Thinking — solving problems, delivering user value
  • —AI Literacy — effective AI use in the design process
Problem Reframe

The real job-to-be-done isn't "see more charts." It's: "Tell me what to do next, and why." Sales Managers need triage, not more visibility. The mental model they already have is email — prioritized, actionable, time-sensitive. That reframe drives every design decision in this project.

Problem Statement
Sales Managers in enterprise B2B waste up to 2 hours daily manually searching through dashboards because the platform delivers data but doesn't interpret it — resulting in delayed responses to deal risks and decisions based on gut feel rather than facts.
Design Bet
DataFlow delivers data, but not decisions. We need to transform the platform from a reporting system into an action system — before the Sales Manager loses another deal they could have saved.
